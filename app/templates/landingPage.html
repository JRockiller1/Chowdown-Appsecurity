Why Us

Why choose our platform?

01

Location

Our vendors are able to deliver food in a fast and timely manner within at least a 15km radius in Singapore.

02

Quality

We guarantee the best quality of food for our valued customers.


We will send one of our food critics to evaluate the food of vendors that applies with us. Only once they have passed our quality check, will they be able to sign up with us.

03

Aiding Smaller vendors

Smaller vendors are more often overlooked by consumers when it comes to picking out food as they are unknown and placed in a remote location such as the void deck.


Due to this, we advocate and deliver for these smaller companies who may not have the branding benefit of common big-named food companies in the industry such as Mc Donalds.

Percy's Pizza's Extravaganza

Margherita pizza is one of the simplest and most traditional pizzas, named after Queen Margherita, the wife of Umberto I. She was said to have appreciated its mild, fresh flavor and to have even noted that its topping colors, green, white, and red resembeles the Italian flag.

Enjoy this delicious dish of Italian origin consists of a flattened disk of bread dough seasoned with olive oil, oregano, tomatoes, olives, mozzarella or cheese, and other ingredients, baked in an authentic wood-fired oven heated to a very high temperature—and served piping hot.

Gabraille's Amazing Rice Combo

Roasted chicken rice is a favorite among the locals in Malaysia and Singapore. Definitely a great dish at any time of the day.

Gabraille's Amazing Rice contains tender, steamed white chicken cut into bite-sized pieces, served with fragrant rice and light soy sauce.The dish is accompanied with a chilli sauce made of chillies, chicken broth, garlic and ginger. In addition, it is recommended to serve chicken stock steeped in a thick broth garnished with spring onions and coriander leaves.Many may not have guessed but the ‘roasted’ chicken is actually fried! Unlike the crunch you'd expect from fried chicken, this dish is fried using a two-step method that makes the skin taste like honey and makes the skin smooth.
